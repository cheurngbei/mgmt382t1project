Our Services



Here at Eloheh, we are committed to providing comprehensive healthcare solutions tailored to meet your needs. Our team of medical professionals will ensure that you have the best experience with us. Below is a list of our core service areas:

Primary Care

Compassionate and consistent care for you and your families:

  • Annual Physical Exams
  • Chronic Disease Management (Diabetes, Hypertension, etc.)
  • Immunizations and Vaccinations
  • Preventive Health Screenings
  • Acute Illness Diagnosis and Treatment

Specialist Services

Expert care across a wide range of specialties:

  • Cardiology
  • Dermatology
  • Orthopedics
  • Gastroenterology
  • Endocrinology

Diagnostic Services

Advanced diagnostic equipment for accurate and timely results:

  • Laboratory Testing (Blood, Urine, etc.)
  • X-Ray Imaging
  • Ultrasound
  • MRI and CT Scans
  • Cardiac Stress Tests

Telehealth

Virtual care when you can't visit us:

  • Virtual Doctor Visits
  • Medication Refills
  • Follow-up Appointments
  • Behavioral Health Counseling
  • Specialist Referrals

Emergency & Urgent Care

24/7 medical support for unexpected health issues:

  • Minor Injuries and Burns
  • Fever, Flu, and Infections
  • Sprains and Fractures
  • Allergic Reactions
  • Shortness of Breath or Chest Pain
